# Profile comparisons

**High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi:** Pop pulls Sunrise City (pop/happy); lofi pulls Library Rain and Midnight Coding (both lofi/chill). The lists differ by genre and target energy, which is what the score is built to optimize.

**High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock:** Pop leads with pop tracks; rock leads with Storm Runner (rock/intense). Intuition matches: different genres pick different anchors.

**Deep Intense Rock vs Edge case (pop + melancholic + high energy):** Rock profile elevates Storm Runner; edge case still rewards pop hits (Gym Hero, Sunrise City) without a melancholic mood match because genre and energy dominate. The edge profile surfaces Cathedral Light only at #3 via mood match with weak energy fit.

**Chill Lofi vs Edge case:** Lofi prefers low-energy lofi rows; edge case prefers high-energy pop and electronic—energy and genre targets diverge, so outputs are almost disjoint.

**Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock:** Lofi top scores are low-energy lofi/chill; rock top is high-energy rock/intense. Mood + genre + energy line up with expected “vibes.”

**High-Energy Pop vs Edge case:** Both share pop at the top, but edge case drops mood-matched happy tracks in favor of raw genre + energy when mood is melancholic—shows how mood is weighted less than genre.
